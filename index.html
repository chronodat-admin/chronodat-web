Help Desk
Chronodat App / Add-In

Chronodat apps/add-in's are powered by Microsoft SharePoint and certified within the Microsoft MarketPlace.

The Chronodat Help Desk add-in provides a seamless integration of Help Desk features, using Office 365 SharePoint Online. The benefits of the add-in are immediate, due to the robust features and user-friendly interfaces it provides. We understand that every business has varying needs, and that is why we offer so much customization within the add-in.

Creating a request and tracking it to its end resolution in Chronodat Help Desk has been crafted with the goal of benefiting the requestor of help desk services. The email notification at each request state delivers the assurance to user that there is progress on their requested tasks.
